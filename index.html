About Us

ANG DONGKA (THAILAND) CO., LTD.

We are seeking international distributors for our premium Thai products. With direct access to high-capacity manufacturing facilities, we are well-equipped to support large-scale global demand. Our diverse product range combines quality, functionality, and competitive pricing—featuring organic ingredients, authentic Thai craftsmanship, and internationally trusted standards. We welcome long-term partnerships with businesses who share our passion for delivering excellence to customers around the world.

We don’t just sell great products, we also serve as a trusted sourcing partner in Thailand. Whether you are looking for specific raw materials or customized goods, we can find and help to handle the entire process—from production oversight and legal compliance, quality control, coordination, and international shipping.

Why work with ANG DONGKA?

We are a company dedicated to supporting local SME, OTOP products from Thailand. We carefully select products made from high-quality, safe ingredients—ideal for health-conscious consumers who value natural alternatives. We aim to support and uplift small-scale producers in rural communities who often lack access to funding, digital marketing expertise, and knowledge of export or international tax procedures, which limits their ability to compete in major markets or expand globally. We serve as a vital bridge, enabling these local products to reach international markets in a structured and sustainable way. Our company visits on-site inspections at manufacturing to verify the identity of their operations and assess their credibility. We prioritize transparency in every step about their process. Evaluating their production capacity for international markets and ensuring that the products offered are reasonably priced. When you do business with us, you can be confident that your products will be delivered reliably and on time. By partnering with us, you not only gain access to premium Thai products but also help support the sustainable growth of Thai SMEs on the global stage.
